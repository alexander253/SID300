Dingo

Distinctive Features

Dingoes have golden brown fur, and resemble very closely to your everyday dog. But make no mistake these aniamls can be dangerous if aggrevated, so make sure to keep your distance.

Diet

Dingoes are carnivorous which means you might see them hanging around areas with smalls mammamls.
